Skeletal sentence

Underlining indicates the main structure of independent and dependent clauses in the primary sentence structure. Highlighting is of the subject, verb, direct object, and adjectives, and adverbs that directly modify any of them. If there are more than one subjects, verbs, or direct objects, marking may be of one or all.

Secondary conjunction
